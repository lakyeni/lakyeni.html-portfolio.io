The best movies according by Enikő

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Law abiting citizen / 2009 (Törvénytisztelő polgár)

The film revolves around a man, Clyde Shelton, who seeks justice through legal means after the brutal murder of his family. When the court delivers a lenient verdict and the killers receive relatively light sentences, Clyde decides to take justice into his own hands. His goal is to punish those responsible by exploiting the flaws in the legal system, targeting not only the murderers but everyone who was indirectly involved in the tragedy.

The Shawshank Redemption / 1994 (A remény rabjai)

The Shawshank Redemption (1994) is a powerful drama based on Stephen King's novella. It follows Andy Dufresne (Tim Robbins), a banker wrongfully convicted of murder, who is sentenced to life in Shawshank prison. Over the years, Andy forms a deep friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding (Morgan Freeman) and finds ways to maintain his hope and dignity despite the harsh environment. The film explores themes of hope, freedom, and redemption as Andy works to escape the confines of both the prison and his past. Widely regarded as one of the greatest films ever made, it celebrates the resilience of the human spirit.

Hidden figures / 2017 (A számolás joga)

Hidden Figures (2016) is a biographical drama that tells the inspiring true story of three African American women mathematicians—Katherine Johnson, Dorothy Vaughan, and Mary Jackson—who played key roles at NASA during the space race. Despite facing racial and gender discrimination, they made groundbreaking contributions to the success of early space missions, including John Glenn's orbital flight. The film highlights their intelligence, determination, and resilience. Hidden Figures is a powerful story of overcoming adversity and breaking barriers. It celebrates the unsung heroes who helped shape history.

Wrath of man / 2021 (Egy igazán dühös ember)

Wrath of Man is a 2021 British-American action thriller directed by Guy Ritchie. The film stars Jason Statham as H, a mysterious man who starts working for an armored truck company. At first, he appears to be a quiet, detached worker, but it soon becomes clear that he has a hidden agenda tied to a personal vendetta. As the story unfolds, H’s intense rage and brutal force come to the forefront as he seeks revenge against those who wronged him. The film is a tense and gripping tale, exploring themes of vengeance and justice with a dark, action-packed edge.
