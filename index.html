Welcome to my Portfolio

Hello!
I'm Enikő, a passionate full-stack developer student. I enjoy creating web applications and learning new technologies. This portfolio showcases some of my projects and skills. I started this journey in 2025 and have been continuously improving my skills. I'm still a beginner, as can be seen in my projects, but I learn quickly, as seen in my recent uploads. I'm excited to share my work with you! Technology has always fascinated me. I've been interested in computers for a long time because there are so many different aspects I find intriguing, from hardware to software. However, I wasn't sure which area I'd be good at. At my workplace, I was tasked with maintaining the company website even though I had no formal qualifications — just 20 years less experience than my colleagues! :D That's when I stumbled into web development, and since we used WordPress, I didn't necessarily need to learn programming languages. Instead, I was working with ready-made templates and maintaining the site's content. In November 2025, I started a Udemy course on full-stack development, which I completed by January 2026, covering HTML and CSS. In January 2026, I began the Prooktatás Full Stack Developer course, and I'm excited to dive into JavaScript and everything else that comes with it.
